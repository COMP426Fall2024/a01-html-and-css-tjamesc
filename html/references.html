In the popular TV series, The Office, Dwight Schrute portrayed Belsnickel at an office Christmas party. He spent most of his night determining if his coworkers were "impish or admirable," a knockoff of "naughty or nice." He gives out German food, treats, and gifts to his admirable coworkers, and coal to his impish coworkers. Dwight even hits Jim repeatedly with his switch when Jim announces he is taking a new job in Philadelphia.
